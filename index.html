What Our Students Have to Say

While studying for STEM entrance exam, I was under high mental pressure. But when I found STEM Mentorship under STEM teachers' supervision and organized by STEM students, I couldn't be more eager to apply. Although the mentorship entrance exam was quite hard, it encouraged me to move forward. The mentors have worked on our points of weakness to develop our problem-solving skills. Finally, I easily tackled STEM entrance exam.
Anas Ahmed
A rising junior at STEM October
When I first read the announcement for STEM mentorship last year, it sounded like a great opportunity to enhance my skills. Even though It was my first time dealing with STEM students, I felt comfortable. They answered our questions patiently and gave us as much as possible about their experience. Certainly, it was not easy to go through all those materials, but with time management skills and patience, I could get through them and pass the admission test successfully.
Mohamed Abdalla
A rising junior at STEM October
Preparing for the STEM entrance exam with a significantly low acceptance rate was not the easiest thing to do. Thankfully, STEM October mentorship offered constructive help in each section. I can't forget the patient mentor and how they were well prepared. Mentors put much effort into the remarkably challenging questions, thus pivoting our problem-solving skills. They changed our view of math problems from easy exercises to challenging ones.
Hassan Ayman
A rising Senior at STEM October-Current President of STEM Mentorship
While preparing for the admission test, I used to depend on self-study. So, I searched for people who could help me to prepare for the admission test. While I was surfing a Whatsapp group chat for students, I came across the STEM October mentorship and decided to join. I remember being not that good at English, especially in reading, but the mentors of the English section truly enhanced my English skills and made me love it from my heart.
Yousef Gamal
A rising junior at STEM October
