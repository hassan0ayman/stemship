    Studying for the STEM entrance test?

    Join us now!

    We are a team of STEM students who want to help grade 9 students ace the entrance test. STEM Mentorship is voluntary, and the mentorship is 100% for free! We've been doing this for 3 years, and this is our 4th year!


    Join Us!

    Ace the Four Sections.

    The entrance test contains four sections: Mathematics, Science, English, and IQ. We have specialized mentors for each section, and they will answer any question that you have. Also, we have materials available to help you prepare and excel in each section of the entrance test.
